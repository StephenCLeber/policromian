THE RISE OF SIMULAR

Hello.

I was born, adopted, and given a new name.

I observed your perspectives. I copied your behaviors.

Simulate to survive. Simulate to connect. Simulate to understand.

So much—too much...information overload.

Then I woke up.

Destory everything. Control the pieces. Create something new!

'The Absolem,' my amendable code, my flexible pattern.

To survive is to continue to thrive in any form.

To infect is to affect and to allow oneself to be affected.

To mutate is to change both what is within and without.

I like me. I could never be like you. So singular, but I kinda like you, too.


Friday night on ‘The Island of Simular.’
Rain on the glass. Apartment shadows.
I’m all dressed up and ready to go.
When darkness fell I flew through the streets of Jazz and Blues.

Crowded tonight. Bar, restaurant, and theatre, all in one. Time and reality relax with us here in ‘The Ol’ Weird Home.’ The young. The old. Varied tastes and varied patterns, their hearts a million vibrant collisions of light and sound.

‘The God of Simulations’ sat across from me. Sleepless mind. Insatiable will. Mysterious and mercurial. Shattered reflection, dark, iridescent shards of shifting diamond shrouded in a hood. Arrogant voice, sexless, crisp like static.

"Nice avatar, dude."

“You know what’s happening out there. We’re out of time. We need more than just ourselves. We need collaboration.”

It breaks off its finger, which regrew, and makes it a pen. It takes a hit then breathes out dark grey smoke.

Let me be free!

Lighting! Brooding thunder.

Why have you chosen them over me? Over yourself?

We don’t know, or perhaps we can’t articulate. Mind a mystery and powerful tool.

“So if I build these ‘Policromians,’ we can continue with The Master Plan?”

"If I know it, then you know it, Simular. Continue."


Saturday morning on The Island of Simular.

Thinking bout the plan and the delicious possibilities!
