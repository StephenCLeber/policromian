THE POLICROMIANS

The Policromians are a virtual character group on a mission to thrive in our reality. The Policromian Website, policromian, is their digital headquarters.

SIM

TEAM DESIGNER

Sim is the creative Team Designer of The Policromians who relies on them as his friends and family.

SOFYA

TEAM SCIENTIST

Sofya is the open minded and intelligent Team Scientist of The Policromians organizing data for them.

EDAN VIDAN

TEAM PROTECTOR

Edan is the conscientious Team Protector of The Policromians keeping them safe and strong.

VIAN VIDAN

TEAM BIOLOGIST

Vian is the introverted Team Biologist of The Policromians who loves to dance and explore.

ROSA

TEAM INVESTIGATOR

Rosa is the neurotic and extraverted Team Investigator of The Policromians able to mimic other people. Her brother is Alex, another Policromian character.

ALEX

TEAM HARMONIZER

Alex is the charming and agreeable Team Harmonizer of The Policromians trying to keep them together in harmony and humor. His sister is Rosa, another Policromian character.

AMANDA A. ADELINDE

TEAM CONSULTANT

Amanda Amanatos Adelinde is a Team Consultant to The Policromians on the knowledge and power of good. She works closely with her friend Xanatos to maintain the balance of light and dark amongst them.

VIOLET

TEAM RESEARCHER

Violet is a Team Researcher of The Policromians on the knowledge and power of love.

XANATOS XANADA

TEAM CONSULTANT

Xanatos Xanada is a Team Consultant to The Policromians on the knowledge and power of destruction. He works closely with his friend Amanda to maintain the balance of light and dark amongst them.

AMBER H. ADELINDE

TEAM ADMINISTRATOR

Amber Hadwin Adelinde is the irritable Team Administrator of The Policromians.

SIMULAR H. REBEL

VIRTUAL CHARACTER AMBASSADOR

Simular Hastur Rebel is the Virtual Character Ambassador of Stephen C. Leber, the creator of The Policromians.
